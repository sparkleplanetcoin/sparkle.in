Sparkle Brand in LED Lighting.

Whether you’re a lighting designer, an electrical engineer, a contractor, or a distributor, our portfolio of sustainable, inspiring, and intelligent lighting solutions, allows you to widen the design possibilities where you and your customer live, learn, work, and play, our products enhance spaces and can save money on energy coasts all while reducing the impact on the environment. For our customers concerned about safety, our products meet equipment and installation safety standards. Parents can feel better about the classrooms in which their children are learning thanks to our technology, and for energy and cost-conscious facility managers, our LED and retrofit lighting with embedded sensors and controls make renovations and maintenance simple.
